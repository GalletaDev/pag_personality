Privacy policies

Cookie Policy
This website uses cookies to improve the user experience and enable the operation of third-party services such as music players, donation buttons, and embedded videos.
What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences, enable certain features, or collect anonymous information about the use of the site.
What cookies do we use?
This site does not directly install its own cookies, but uses third-party services that may set cookies in your browser:
Bandcamp
To play music from our site. Bandcamp may use cookies to improve the performance of the player.
Ko-Fi
If you decide to support us through Ko-fi, this platform may use cookies to manage your session or process payments.
Paypal
When making a donation or purchase, PayPal may place cookies for security and fraud detection purposes.
Youtube
Some content includes embedded YouTube videos. This platform may use cookies for statistics and content personalization.
How can you control cookies?
You can manage or delete cookies from your browser settings. You can also browse in incognito mode or use extensions that block third-party cookies. Your privacy is important, and as a developer, it is my duty not to involve you in anything.
